The Chronicle of Adellar

Born in Daggerford to Llwellyn Longhand, the town's master of arms, and Maedra Varna Shadowdusk, a noblewoman of Waterdeep — Adellar's birth was never simple. The Shadowdusk line had already traded away pieces of itself to something called the Pale Lady, and Maedra had been sent to Daggerford to secure a foothold, not to fall in love. She did both.

When Waterdeep's authorities finally moved against House Shadowdusk's corruption, Maedra fled into Undermountain with her infant son to complete a ritual meant to make him a vessel. The raid arrived in time to save his life, but not in time to undo what had been done.

He grew up ordinary, gifted at conjuration, until the Time of Troubles tore his mind loose and flung it into the Far Realm — while the Pale Lady wore his body for five years. When Adellar returned, he had lost his father to assassins, his estate to creditors, and any clean memory of who he'd been. He gave what was left of himself to Shar, and now walks half a man, half a key, waiting to learn what door he opens.
